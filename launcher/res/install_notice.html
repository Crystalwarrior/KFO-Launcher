Welcome to Killing Fever Online!

Chat with animated characters, backgrounds, sounds and music - create immersive and fun experiences with fellow role-players!

There are a variety of active servers with their own communities. Please be sure to follow their rules, and, if applicable, download their base packs, which may be found in each server's description.

Disclaimer

Some servers may offer custom content; these assets remain the property of their respective copyright holders.

When you join a server, your hard drive's ID may be stored by the server as a countermeasure for ban evasion. In-character and out-of-character chat may also be logged for moderation purposes. Please contact the server's admin for further inquiries about privacy.
